About Brand Hunt

A seasoned expert in high-performance computing and low-latency solutions, Brand Hunt has a rich history of driving technological innovation. As the President & Founder of 60East Technologies, he leads the development of the Advanced Message Processing System (AMPS), a cutting-edge platform designed for high throughput and minimal latency.

His entrepreneurial spirit also led him to found the Las Vegas Circus Center, showcasing a unique blend of technical leadership and creative vision. With a background that includes key roles at Merrill Lynch, Morgan Stanley, and IBM, and a publication credit for O'Reilly's "SQL in a Nutshell," Brand's expertise is both deep and diverse. He holds a B.S. in Computer Science from Oregon State University and is an inventor on multiple patents related to data processing and real-time systems.
